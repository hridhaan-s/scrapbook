# scrapbook
The archive of everything I've built, broken, learned and shipped.

A personal collection of projects, experiments, open-source contributions, hackathon builds, websites, tools, and ideas — documented as they happened.

Unlike a traditional portfolio, Scrapbook focuses on the journey as much as the outcome. Every entry captures what was built, why it existed, what worked, what failed, and what was learned along the way.

## Philosophy

Projects are more than screenshots and links.


This repository serves as a long-term engineering journal for me and public archive.

## Contents

Entries may include:

* Personal projects
* Open-source contributions
* Hack Club work
* School projects
* Hackathon submissions
* Experiments and prototypes
* Failed ideas worth remembering

## Structure

```text
content/
├── project-1.mdx
├── project-2.mdx
└── project-3.mdx

public/
└── images/

app/
└── ...
```

Each project is stored as an individual Markdown/MDX file with metadata, screenshots, links, and notes.

## Tech Stack
-HTML
-CSS
-JS

## Why This Exists

Most projects disappear after they are finished.

Scrapbook exists so they don't.

Years from now, I want to be able to look back and see every version of myself through the things I built.
