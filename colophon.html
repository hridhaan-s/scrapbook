About the archive

My main site keeps things tidy — only the projects I'm most proud of. This scrapbook is the messy, honest version: everything I've ever built, in one place, in black and white.

Each entry has a thumbnail, a short note, the languages I used, and where the project lived in my life — school, a hackathon, a weekend experiment, a thing for a friend.

Most entries link back to GitHub, live demos, or archived screenshots. Some projects are polished. Some are weird experiments. Both deserve a place here.

Only I can add or edit entries — the rest of the world just reads.
